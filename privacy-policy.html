Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Joker’s Sparkle Party, published by Digital Excellence Apps Development Studio, handles user information.

Data Collection

We do not collect personal data, device data, usage data, or any other user information through this app.

Data Storage

We do not store user data on our servers, in external databases, or in any private storage systems controlled by us.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, transfer, or disclose user data to third parties because we do not collect such data in the first place.

Children’s Privacy

Since no personal information is collected, no personal information from children is collected, stored, or shared by this app.

Third-Party Services

At the current stage, this app is intended to operate without collecting or transmitting personal user information through the developer. If a future version introduces features that change this privacy model, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features are made available.

Current privacy position: no data collection, no data storage, no data transfer.
Transparency commitment: any future privacy-related change will be publicly reflected on this page.
